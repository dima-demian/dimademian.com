EnglishPrime

The key visual made for the English language school ranked #1 in the world for the last 5 years. The main purpose is to use that image in promotion capaigns, social media, advertising activity and education materials.

The creation

The concept centres around combining the UK flag with a crossroads to symbolise the power of choice and freedom. To bring this idea to life, a 3D image crafted and then merged with a photo. Finally, the image a meticulous finishing touch given.
The idea belongs to Luca Guarini (DLV BBDO, Milan)
